THE FUTURE OF DESIGN

We have designed a program to help designers define and improve their fashion value chain. From how you design, brand yourself and the product, to how you get you and your product to the market. This programme is for upcoming fashion designers with a focus on strategically turning their talent into a sustainable venture. Our dedicated team, partners and mentors deliver key insights on what it takes to strategize in Branding, Design and Distribution. This 8-week intensive program is every designer's stepping stone to market readiness!
